Organize Your Team

You will need 4 or 5 people to work each day that your team volunteers.

- One person is needed to pick up the food from the restaurant at about 11:20am so they can be at the soup kitchen by 11:30am with the food.

- Someone will open the kitchen, prepare coffee & juice & set out supplies. They should be there at about 11:30am.

- During the lunch hour about 4 people are needed .... this will most likley include the pickup & prep people who will stay through the lunch hour.

- There will be one or two behind the counter dishing up soup, etc. The other two will serve the meals to guests, offer refreshments and wait on tables.

- One or two volunteers need to stay behind to wash tables, clean floors and bathrooms. They should be able to be finished by 1:30 or 2:00pm

If you have any questions, please contact one of our volunteer co-ordinators.
